Importance of Salah

Prayer is one of the greatest pillars of Islam. It strengthens our connection with Allah Almighty and brings peace to the heart.

Fajr

Start your day with the remembrance of Allah.

Dhuhr

Pause your work and worship Allah.

Asr

Protect your faith through regular prayers.

Maghrib

Thank Allah for the blessings of the day.

Isha

End your day with prayer and gratitude.

Reminder
